Romi Jin

Romi Jin is currently a freshman pursuing a Bachelor of Architecture at Carnegie Mellon University. She is from New York City but currently lives in Pittsburgh. She is intrigued in the overlap and application of architecture to a plethora of other subjects and its potential role as a catalyst for change in our society. She especially loves to solve problems, whether it is a mathematics equation, defining spatial qualities, or relating architecture to the physical site and surrounding environment. Her hobbies include photography, drawing, reading, travelling, and eating delicious food.
